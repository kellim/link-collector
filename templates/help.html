Help

Collections, Categories, and Links

Links are contained in categories, and categories are all part of a more general collection. For instance, you might have a collection called Recipes, and within that collection you have categories like Breakfast, Entrees, and Desserts. You would then add links to breakfast recipes in the Breakfast category, and links to desserts in the Desserts category, etc.

Navigating the Site

Once you click on a collection on the main page, it'll take you to a page that lists categories with links. You'll start out on the default category, but can choose a different category from the sidebar. You can also choose a different collection from the sidebar.

Logging in to the Site

Click the Login button in the top navigation bar to go to the login page that has your options for logging in.

Currently, you can login to the site with a Google+ or a Facebook account. If you do not have either of these types of accounts, you'll need to obtain one before you can login to this site.

Once you have logged in, you'll see a Logout button in place of the Login button.

Adding, Editing, and Deleting Links

To add a link, you'll need to login first. Once you login and choose a collection, you'll be on the page that lists links for a category. This page will have an Add New Link button at the bottom which will allow you to add a link.

Once you add a link, it will show up in the list of links with a pencil icon and a trash can icon next to it. The pencil icon will take you to a form where you can edit the link. The trash can icon will delete the link once you confirm you want to delete it.

Please add only quality links that pertain to the category they are posted in. They must also not be spam or promote hatred or anything illegal. Links that do not meet these standards will be removed from the site.

Issues, Suggestions, Etc.

If you have any issues with the site, or have suggestions—such as collections or categories you'd like to see added to the site—click the contact link in the top navigation bar to contact the site developer/admin about it.
